Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

Tasker is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best task management experience:

  • Task Data: Your tasks, projects, and productivity metrics are stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to improve app performance (can be disabled in Settings)
  • Crash Reports: Technical diagnostics to fix bugs and improve stability

How We Use Your Information

  • Sync your tasks across your Apple devices using iCloud
  • Provide productivity analytics and insights
  • Improve app performance and fix issues
  • Send you important updates about the app (if you opt-in)

Data Storage & Security

Your personal task data is stored securely:

  • Local Storage: All data is encrypted and stored locally on your device
  • iCloud Sync: Data syncing uses Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure
  • No Third-Party Access: We never share your personal task data with third parties

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Disable analytics in Settings › Privacy
  • Export your data via Settings › Data Export
  • Request data deletion by contacting support

Third-Party Services

Tasker may use the following services:

  • Apple iCloud: For data synchronization (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Apple Analytics: For crash reporting and performance metrics

Children's Privacy

Tasker is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
