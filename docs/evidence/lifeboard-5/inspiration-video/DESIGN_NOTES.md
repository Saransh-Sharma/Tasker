# Inspiration video design notes

Source: `INSPIRATION APP VIDEO.mp4` (private design reference; not shipped)

## Composition cues retained

- One warm scenic header carries personality; the operational list stays on a quiet cream plane.
- Greeting, date, and four small progress signals form a compact first read rather than a wall of metrics.
- Medication/care actions use a restrained two-column tile grid with generous tap targets.
- Tasks remain open rows. Secondary actions live in menus and swipe/context interactions rather than persistent buttons.
- Section headers are short and left aligned, with small trailing add controls.
- Warm cocoa type and pale gold accents create hierarchy without introducing system blue.

## Motion cues adapted

- Context menus originate from the touched item and dismiss back into it.
- Completion is local and brief; the row remains understandable before moving.
- Progress rings animate only when their real value changes.
- Editing uses native sheets with stable geometry instead of transforming the entire screen.

## Deliberate LifeBoard refinements

- The reference uses dense card outlines and small text in places. LifeBoard increases target sizes, Dynamic Type resilience, and open whitespace.
- Glass is limited to navigation, composer, filters, and transient controls. Content remains opaque paper.
- Zero, stale, unavailable, setup-required, and permission states are visually distinct.
- Continuous animation and rotating controls are excluded; the five named shader moments remain bounded and meaningful.

The generated contact sheet and individual frames in this directory are non-shipping evidence only and must never be added to an application target.
